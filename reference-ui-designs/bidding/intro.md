I've designed a suite of premium, role-specific screens for the Ameefar Energy bidding and trade workflow. This flow follows the "Trust-First" architecture, clearly separating the negotiation (Enquiry) and execution (Trade) phases for both Buyers and Sellers.

Bidding & Trade Flow Overview:
Buyer: Negotiation Room (Buyer: Negotiation Room (Enquiry Phase)): A real-time hub for managing active offers. It features a dual-panel layout where the buyer can track current terms (Quantity, Price, Incoterms) and counter-offers while communicating directly with the seller through an integrated chat.
Seller: Negotiation Room (Seller: Negotiation Room (Enquiry Phase)): Optimized for the seller's first response. It highlights the buyer's trust score and verification status alongside the initial offer, with clear actions to accept, decline, or issue a counter-proposal.
Seller: Trade Execution (Seller: Trade Execution Room (Trade Phase)): Once a bid is accepted, the trade moves to this structured execution room. It guides the seller through the "Happy Path" via a visual stepper—starting with contract uploads and progressing toward logistics and completion.
Buyer: Trade Management (Buyer: Trade Management & Fulfillment): Provides the buyer with total visibility during fulfillment. This includes a logistics tracking module, access to essential documents (Contracts, Bill of Lading), and a dedicated inspection module to verify material quality before settlement.
All screens are unified by the Ameefar Core design system, utilizing the deep navy palette (#0f3d3e), professional Hanken Grotesk typography, and your official brand logo to maintain a high-trust, enterprise-grade experience.
